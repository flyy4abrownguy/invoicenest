===========================================
NOTES
===========================================

1. All NEXT_PUBLIC_* variables are exposed to the browser
2. Keep service role keys and secrets private
3. After adding variables, redeploy for changes to take effect
4. Test cron jobs after deployment:
   - /api/cron/generate-recurring-invoices
   - /api/cron/send-payment-reminders
5. Vercel automatically adds Authorization header with CRON_SECRET for cron jobs
